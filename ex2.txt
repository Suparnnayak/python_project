The sky was sailed across by the dragon, and enormous shadows were cast by its wings on the emerald forest below.
Meanwhile, mead was being sipped by a small group of adventurers in a dusty tavern, and rumors of hidden treasures deep in the mountains were being discussed.
Somewhere, the passage of time was relentlessly ticked by a clock, unaware of the strange world for which time was being kept, 
where magic and mystery were woven through the very fabric of existence like threads in a forgotten tapestry.
